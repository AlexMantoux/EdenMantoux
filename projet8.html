Nuclear energy

UE : Outils/Applications

Matière : Anglais

For years, public opinion has been divided on the question of nuclear power as an energy of the future. By disseminating accurate information and dispelling misinformation, the media has helped to alleviate fears surrounding nuclear energy. As a result, public perception has shifted, with nuclear power now being seen as a form of green energy. To what extent the transition to clean energy is conceivable, ensuring the fulfillment of needs while also considering the sustainability of nuclear energy.
